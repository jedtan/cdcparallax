CHANGE IS HAPPENING

The Career Development Center has recently transformed into Stanford Career Education and is now known as BEAM (Bridging Education, Ambition and Meaningful Work). At BEAM, connections are at the heart of our model. By empowering connections, we create a culture of happenstance to help bridge students' education, ambition and meaningful work. Celebrate this change with us on January 14, 2016.

STANFORD'S CAREER CONNECTIONS MODEL

The Career Connections Model focuses on developing clear bridges between all of the people, entities and knowledge needed to form an opportunity ecosystem – a web of career connections – that students can then tap into to achieve goal resolution and find satisfying, meaningful work.
