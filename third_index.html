CHANGE IS HAPPENING

The Career Development Center has recently transformed into Stanford Career Education, and is now known as BEAM (Bridging Education, Ambition, and Meaningful Work). At BEAM, connections are the heart of our model. By empowering connections, we create a culture of happenstance to help bridge students' education, ambition, and meaningful work.
