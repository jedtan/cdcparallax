Stanford is transforming how students transition from university to career. Formally known as the Stanford Career Development Center, we are now Stanford BEAM Career Education. We are empowering students to catalyze connections everywhere they go. These connections build their opportunity ecosystem and shape their journey towards finding meaningful work. THE

VISION

All students transform their ambitions into meaningful work over the course of their lifetime. "Stanford's model of career connections rely on the concept of meaningful work, because it's different. It's not just about helping students get jobs, it's about positioning students to obtaining the type of work that adds value and meaning to their own life..." - Farouk Dey, Associate Vice Provost of Student Affairs & Dean of Career Education

anchor

OUR

MISSION

We empower students to cultivate personalized networks that shape their professional journey. We do this by focusing on developing clear bridges between all the people, partners, stakeholders, entitites and knowledge needed to form an opportunity ecosystem that will help students achieve goal resolution and find satisfying meaningful work.
